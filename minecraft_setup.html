Minecraft Setup Tutorial:

1. Where to buy Minecraft Java Edition

2. Installing Minecraft

3. Connecting to the Server


1. Where to buy Minecraft Java Edition

1. Go to https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/store/minecraft-java-edition

2. Click on "Buy Minecraft".

3. Sign in to your hotmail/Microsoft account (or create one!)

4. Follow the steps to buy Minecraft.


2. Installing Minecraft

0. If you haven't bought Minecraft yet, the later steps of this section won't work! Click on me to go back!

1. Go to Minecraft's Download page. Here's a link to it: https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/download

2. Install the game and follow the steps.

3. Open the Minecraft launcher. Here's what you should see:

4. Sign in to your Microsoft account that you bought Minecraft with.

5. Once the launcher looks like this, click on the giant green "PLAY" button.

6. The download will take 5-10 minutes depending on your internet speed.


3. Connecting to the Server

1. The game should open from the launcher right after the download from the previous step. If not, open the game.

2. Click on "Multiplayer".

3. Yada yada. Click on the checkbox and "Proceed".

4. Allow communication on private networks, then "Allow Access".

5. We're finally on the crucial part. Click on "Add Server".

6. Copy the "Server Address" exactly as it is. You can keep the "Server Name" too if you want :')

7. Click on "Done". The server will be added, then you can click on it and then "Join Server"

8. Tada! You made it!

P.S. Here's a guide to controls if you are new to the game: http://www.minecraft101.net/g/controls-and-keyboard.html
